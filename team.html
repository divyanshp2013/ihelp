Academic Vertical

All children have dreams, and the right to strive for them. But few have the means to achieve them. The Academic Vertical works to attain equality of opportunities by bridging the gap. We aim at making the children capable of achieving their dreams and reaching their goals. With the help of uniquely crafted booklets, worksheets and video modules, we provide them with a creative learning experience. We not only focus on their academic progress but also their wholesome development. By implementing engaging teaching methodologies, we ensure that they are able to learn step by step, and they are able to use the learnings in their everyday life. Our goal this year is to reach out to as many children as we can and help them discover and realise their true potential. We wish to enable them to contribute to society and future developments

Human Resource & Operations Vertical

From the scheduling of the visits to organising various events and everything in between, the HR and Operations vertical does it all. If there's anything that i-Help wants, this vertical is the one they call. Tasks, like collecting feedback, maintaining attendance and communicating with everyone, are what we fulfil while making sure that morale doesn't fall. We may work behind the scenes, but we sure are the backbone of it all.

Projects Team

Here at Projects, we aspire to dive deep into a multitude of problems faced by the stakeholders of i-Help. We evaluate the effectiveness of i-Help's interventions in the past and explore new possibilities to enhance our students' learning experience. Though 'Qualitative' and 'Quantitative' are words we use on a daily basis, we aren't really sophisticated. We love all ideas and want the support of all our volunteers in making our research projects for the year 2020-21 a grand success.
