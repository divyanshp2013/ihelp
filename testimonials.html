Faculty Speaks

"i-Help is a student driven initiative at IIM Indore, underpinned by a firm belief in empowerment of India’s underprivileged children, so as to not only enable them to differentiate between right and wrong but also to help the nation in tapping their unexplored potential. We incessantly endeavour to employ creative learning methods to infuse impressionable young minds with curiosity and excitement as these first generation pupils take their unprecedented steps in embarking upon a journey that will lead to their liberation and our contentment.

We supplement their classroom sessions with creative educational and extracurricular activities at the IIM campus to equip them to think out of the box and facilitate their smooth absorption into the mainstream core. I feel tremendously honoured to be a part of this noble initiative and privileged to work with my student colleagues arduously determined in fulfilling their parts as socially responsible citizens."

-Prof. Surbhi Dayal

Founder Speaks

Kitna bologe bhai

Abhi content change karne ke fucks nahi hai to ye lo copy pasted content. i-Help, a student-run initiative, is an organization of dedicated, like-minded and hardworking individuals who believe that education is the greatest weapon against all evils of society. It’s with this spirit that we associate with various schools and NGOs to assist them with resourceful manpower which has a background from all three streams (Science, Commerce & Arts) to educate, motivate and inculcate the desire to study among those who have been unfortunate enough not to reap the benefits of education. Our primary cause is education of underprivileged children, since we believe that education is the key to overall development of an individual

Coordinator Speaks (2020-21)

Kitna bologe bhai

The smile and feeling of accomplishment on the face of a child when he successfully speaks English and solves a problem in Mathematics passes positive vibes onto one. This is what I experienced and enjoyed as a volunteer of i-Help in my first year of college. Since then i-Help has been very close to my heart. This year I have got the opportunity to bring much more impact on lives of these children. The organisation has grown dynamically since the beginning and I aim to take it a step ahead this year as the Coordinator. The responsibility of 300+ students and 100+ volunteers does feel heavy on my shoulders, but the smile of even one student is worth all of it.

Samyukta - IPM 2016

Dreams, as everyone says, are the most amazing experiences that anyone can have. I started my journey at IIM Indore to be a successful manager because it was my dream. But between the hectic schedule of college; visiting schools, teaching and playing with kids helped me realised that I can act as a channel to fulfill dreams of underprivileged kids. With this in mind, Initially, I joined i-Help as an Acad member, from a member to media co-coordinator, and now overall coordinator of i-Help; I have collected some priceless memories. i-Help as a team has always worked hard to raise funds and make every possible effort for organizing events for these kids, buying them books and what not. We have been successful in making teaching and learning fascinating by use of tablets and laptops and have come a long way ahead from where we started. This year I hope that we bring more smiles and a more significant impact in lives of kids we teach, by scaling up our reach.

Bhavna Giri - IPM 2016
